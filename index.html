The short version Full story →

I've been called a digital unicorn: a product person who can design the interface, write the brief, run the discovery, and ship the thing.

I started in graphic design and IT support, moved into digital production at agencies, built SolarCity's website and lead generators from scratch, and then owning a portfolio of 8–10 products at a global security systems company, spanning firmware, mobile apps, APIs, and web portals simultaneously.

Along the way I've built AR activations before AR was a product category, run customer discovery programmes, integrated AI models across Analytics, App Store Connect, Salesforce, and Zendesk, and navigated stakeholder alignment across engineering, sales, marketing, support, executive, and regional teams on four continents.

I'm not a specialist who drifted into management. I'm someone who has stayed hands-on across the full digital stack - I watch videos on AI for fun - and I think that makes me a fundamentally different kind of product person.
